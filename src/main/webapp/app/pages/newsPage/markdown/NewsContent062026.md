- Registered users may now take advantage of the OncoKB™ Annotator v4.0 tool to annotate germline variant datasets\! Please note however, not all somatic annotation formats have corresponding germline support. Germline annotation is currently available for supported HGVSg, genomic location, and HGVSc query formats only. Make sure to read all changes [here](https://github.com/oncokb/oncokb-annotator#v40).
- Trastuzumab deruxtecan (T-DXd) is [FDA-approved in non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) with activating HER2 (_ERBB2_) mutations](https://www.accessdata.fda.gov/drugsatfda_docs/label/2025/761139s032s035lbl.pdf), as detected by an FDA-approved test. To align with the FDA-approved approved test ([companion diagnostic](https://www.thermofisher.com/us/en/home/clinical/diagnostic-testing/condition-disease-diagnostics/oncology-diagnostics/oncomine-dx-target-test/oncomine-dx-target-test-us-only.html)) for T-DXd , the Level 1 annotation for _ERBB2_ alterations has been updated from _ERBB2 _[Oncogenic Mutations](https://www.oncokb.org/gene/ERBB2/somatic/Oncogenic%20Mutations) to **_ERBB2_** [\*\*Oncogenic Missense Mutations](https://www.oncokb.org/gene/ERBB2/somatic/Oncogenic%20Missense%20Mutations)** and **[Exon 20 In-Frame Insertions (770_831ins)](https://www.oncokb.org/gene/ERBB2/somatic/Exon%2020%20in-frame%20insertions)\** and *ERBB2\* fusions and in-frame insertions outside exon 20 are no longer considered Level 1 alterations in NSCLC.
